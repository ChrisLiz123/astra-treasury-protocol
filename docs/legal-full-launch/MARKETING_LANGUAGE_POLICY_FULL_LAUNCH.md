# Marketing Language Policy for Full-Launch Review

## Approved current language

- AstraTreasury Base Mainnet contracts are deployed in restricted operational mode.
- Restricted capabilities remain disabled.
- No public token sale is approved.
- No real treasury funding is approved.
- No staking, rewards, buybacks, or autonomous execution are approved.
- Mainnet execution queue remains disabled.

## Prohibited language until separately approved

- Public sale is live.
- Buy now.
- Investment opportunity.
- Passive income.
- Guaranteed returns.
- Risk-free.
- Treasury is funded.
- Staking is live.
- Rewards are live.
- Buybacks are active.
- Autonomous execution is live.
- Trading bot is live.
- Full launch is complete.

## Rule

Marketing language must be reviewed before any public full-launch statement.
