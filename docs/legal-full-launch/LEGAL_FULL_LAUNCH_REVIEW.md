# Legal Full-Launch Review

## Current status

Legal full-launch approval: NOT APPROVED

Current approved mode: restricted Base Mainnet operation.

## Purpose

Prepare the legal review package for any future move beyond restricted operation.

## Review areas

- Securities-law treatment.
- Money-transmission / AML / KYC implications.
- Sanctions compliance.
- Tax and accounting treatment.
- Consumer and risk disclosures.
- Marketing language.
- Jurisdictional restrictions.
- Treasury funding structure.
- Token distribution.
- Market/provider listings.

## Still not approved

- Public token sale.
- Real treasury funding.
- Staking or rewards.
- Buyback program.
- Mainnet execution queue.
- Paper-to-on-chain automation.
- Autonomous execution.
- Full launch.

## Rule

No restricted capability may be enabled until counsel and governance complete a separate approval process.
