# Legal Full-Launch Blockers

The following block full launch until resolved:

- Legal full-launch review incomplete.
- Securities-law treatment not approved.
- AML/KYC/money-transmission review incomplete.
- Sanctions controls not reviewed.
- Tax/accounting review incomplete.
- Public disclosures not approved.
- Marketing language not approved.
- Jurisdictional restrictions not approved.
- Token distribution not approved.
- Treasury funding structure not approved.

## Rule

Restricted launch stabilization is not legal approval for full launch.
