# Treasury Source Confidentiality Policy

## Purpose

Prevent accidental publication of sensitive treasury funding information.

## Do not publish

- Bank account numbers.
- Wire instructions.
- Private wallet-control procedures.
- Private keys.
- Seed phrases.
- Signer personal information.
- Private compliance notes.
- Non-public counterparty data.
- Non-public treasury balances.
- Sensitive entity documents.

## Public-safe information

- Source review status.
- Whether funding is approved.
- Whether a funding transaction is authorized.
- Whether risk limits are approved.
- Whether required controls are complete.
- General asset category only after approval.

## Rule

Public pages should only expose sanitized source review status.
