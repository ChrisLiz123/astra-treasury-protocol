# DEX Liquidity Treasury Funding Transfer Approval

## Purpose

Record approval for the DEX liquidity funding-transfer plan based on the reviewed token shortfalls.

## Current status

Funding transfer requirements reviewed: yes.

Funding transfer approval: pending or recorded.

Funding transfer payload generated: no.

Funding transfer executed: no.

Treasury funds moved: no.

Token approval executed: no.

Liquidity added: no.

Position minted: no.

Public trading approved: no.

Full launch approved: no.

## What this approval can approve

- The reviewed funding source reference.
- The reviewed funding source address.
- The destination Safe.
- The token shortfalls calculated in the requirements review.
- Proceeding toward a later funding-transfer payload-generation approval milestone.

## What this approval does not approve

- Funding-transfer calldata generation.
- Funding-transfer Safe payload generation.
- Funding-transfer submission.
- Funding-transfer execution.
- Treasury funds movement.
- Token approval execution.
- Liquidity addition.
- Position minting.
- Public trading.
- Buy page activation.
- Full launch.

## Rule

Funding transfer approval is not funding transfer execution.
