# Global Treasury Funding Approval

## Purpose

Record formal approval for global treasury funding after full launch live/public finalization.

## Current status after this milestone

Full launch live: yes.

Buy page activated: yes.

Public trading live: yes.

Global treasury funding approval: recorded.

Global treasury funding approved: yes.

Funding payload generated: no.

Treasury funding executed: no.

Funds moved: no.

## What this approval can approve

- Proceeding to a later global treasury funding requirements review.
- Proceeding to later funding payload generation after requirements review.
- Proceeding to later Safe submission/execution milestones only after separate approvals.

## What this approval does not do

- Generate funding payloads.
- Submit Safe transactions.
- Execute funding.
- Move funds.

## Rule

Approval is not execution and is not fund movement.
