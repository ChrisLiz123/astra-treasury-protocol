# AstraTreasury Legal Review Brief

This document is for counsel review. It is not legal advice.

## Current project status

AstraTreasury Protocol is a Base Sepolia testnet prototype for AI-assisted treasury governance.

Current state:

- No mainnet launch
- No real treasury funds
- No public token sale
- No investment product
- No promise of returns
- Testnet ASTP has no real monetary value

## Planned review areas

### Securities law

- Token distribution structure
- Public messaging
- Treasury buyback language
- Investment contract risk
- Team allocation and vesting language

### Commodity and market conduct

- Treasury trading behavior
- Buyback disclosures
- Liquidity support disclosures
- Anti-manipulation risk

### Money transmission and custody

- Whether any operator accepts or transmits value for others
- Whether any admin wallet or treasury role creates custody risk
- Whether any future exchange, swap, or reward feature changes obligations

### Consumer protection

- AI limitations
- Testnet versus mainnet disclosures
- Risk disclosures
- No-return-promise language

### Tax and accounting

- Treasury accounting
- Contributor grants
- Token rewards
- Team allocations

## Marketing language to avoid

- Guaranteed returns
- Passive income
- AI profit machine
- Guaranteed buybacks
- Risk-free treasury
- Self-pumping token

## Safer project framing

AstraTreasury is a testnet prototype for AI-assisted treasury governance, transparent signal logging, policy validation, and human-approved execution workflows.
