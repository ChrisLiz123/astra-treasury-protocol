# Compliance Notes

This is not legal advice. It is a project-design note to help keep the MVP conservative.

## Messaging to avoid

- Guaranteed returns.
- Guaranteed passive income.
- Guaranteed buybacks.
- Promises that the token will increase in price.
- Claims that AI can eliminate market risk.

## Safer framing

AstraTreasury Protocol uses AI-assisted risk signals and transparent treasury rules to manage liquidity, reserves, and ecosystem funding.

## Mainnet readiness checklist

- Legal review for token distribution, marketing, custody, treasury operation, and jurisdictional exposure.
- Securities/commodities/MSB analysis.
- Sanctions screening and wallet-control policy where applicable.
- Audit of smart contracts and operational controls.
- Public disclosures for treasury policy, risks, and model limitations.
