# Governance Decision Recording Live Runbook

## Step 1

Confirm the live precheck status is READY_TO_RECORD.

## Step 2

Confirm the action-specific approval record exists.

## Step 3

Confirm signed resolution evidence is imported and validated.

## Step 4

Record the governance decision.

## Step 5

Regenerate public status pages and archive evidence.

## Rule

Do not record a decision if any restricted capability is enabled or approved unexpectedly.
