# DEX Liquidity Provision Approval

## Purpose

Record approval to proceed into liquidity-provision planning and later liquidity Safe payload preparation.

## Current status

Pool created: yes.

Pool verified: yes.

Pool liquidity: zero.

Liquidity provision approval: pending or recorded.

Liquidity added: no.

Treasury funds moved: no.

Public trading approved: no.

Buy page activated: no.

Full launch approved: no.

## What this approval can approve

- Moving from pool-created/no-liquidity state into the liquidity-provision preparation path.
- Later review of token approval requirements.
- Later review of liquidity mint parameters.
- Later review of slippage, deadline, recipient, and Safe transaction path.

## What this approval does not approve

- Token approvals.
- Liquidity Safe payload generation.
- Safe submission.
- Safe execution.
- Liquidity addition.
- Treasury funding.
- Treasury funds movement.
- Public trading.
- Buy page activation.
- Full launch.

## Rule

Liquidity provision approval is not liquidity provision.
