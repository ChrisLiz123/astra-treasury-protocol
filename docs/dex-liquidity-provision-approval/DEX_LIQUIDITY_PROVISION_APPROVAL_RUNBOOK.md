# DEX Liquidity Provision Approval Runbook

## Step 1

Confirm post-execution pool verification is complete.

## Step 2

Confirm pool liquidity is zero.

## Step 3

Confirm treasury funding and full launch remain not approved.

## Step 4

Record liquidity provision approval with a real approver/reference.

## Step 5

Regenerate public status and archive evidence.

## Step 6

Proceed to token approval requirements and liquidity mint parameter review.

## Rule

Do not add liquidity during this approval step.
