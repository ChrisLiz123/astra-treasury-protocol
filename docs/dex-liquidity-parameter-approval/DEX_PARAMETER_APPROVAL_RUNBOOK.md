# DEX Parameter Approval Runbook

## Step 1

Confirm imported DEX parameter selection is valid.

## Step 2

Confirm DEX parameter finalization review is ready.

## Step 3

Record parameter-only approval with a real approver/reference.

## Step 4

Regenerate public status.

## Step 5

Archive evidence.

## Step 6

Proceed to the next dedicated path, such as liquidity source / Safe impact approval.

## Rule

Do not create a pool or add liquidity during this approval step.
