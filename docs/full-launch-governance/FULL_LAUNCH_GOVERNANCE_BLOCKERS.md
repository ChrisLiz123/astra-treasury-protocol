# Full-Launch Governance Blockers

The following block full-launch governance approval:

- Final governance approver not assigned.
- Governance vote or resolution not completed.
- Final decision record not signed.
- Capability activation matrix not approved.
- Treasury risk limits not approved.
- Funding source not approved.
- Treasury Safe approval not recorded.
- Treasury disclosures not final-approved.
- Safe transaction payload not generated or reviewed.
- Public status update not reviewed.

## Rule

Governance decision preparation is not governance approval.
