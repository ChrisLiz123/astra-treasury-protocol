# AstraTreasury Combined Audit and Legal Governance Gate

## Purpose

Prevent mainnet launch, real treasury funding, public token distribution, buyback programs, staking programs, or investor-facing marketing until both external audit and legal review are complete.

## Current status

Network: Base Sepolia
Mainnet launched: no
Real treasury funds: no
Public token sale: no
Investment product: no
Governance gate status: BLOCKING_MAINNET

## Required before mainnet

- External audit completed.
- Audit findings remediated or formally accepted.
- Legal review completed.
- Legal issues resolved or formally accepted.
- Safe multisig role plan finalized.
- Incident response runbook approved.
- Dedicated production RPC plan complete.
- Mainnet go/no-go checklist complete.

## Hard blockers

- Any open CRITICAL audit finding.
- Any open HIGH audit finding.
- Missing legal review.
- Missing multisig role plan.
- Missing incident response plan.
- Any public language implying guaranteed returns.

## Rule

Mainnet is blocked unless this governance gate is explicitly marked PASS by authorized reviewers.
