# Mainnet Blocker Register

## Purpose

Track blockers that prevent mainnet launch.

| ID | Category | Blocker | Status | Owner | Resolution Required | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| BLOCKER-001 | Audit | External audit not completed | OPEN | AstraTreasury | Final audit report and remediation review | Required before mainnet |
| BLOCKER-002 | Legal | Legal review not completed | OPEN | AstraTreasury | Written legal review and go/no-go comments | Required before mainnet |
| BLOCKER-003 | Governance | Real Safe addresses not finalized | OPEN | AstraTreasury | Governance, treasury, executor, and signaler Safe records | Required before mainnet |
| BLOCKER-004 | Infrastructure | Dedicated Base Mainnet RPC not configured | OPEN | AstraTreasury | Primary and backup RPC providers | Required before mainnet |
| BLOCKER-005 | Operations | Mainnet incident response not rehearsed | OPEN | AstraTreasury | Completed rehearsal record | Required before mainnet |

## Status values

- OPEN
- IN_PROGRESS
- RESOLVED
- ACCEPTED_RISK

## Rule

No OPEN blocker may remain before mainnet.
