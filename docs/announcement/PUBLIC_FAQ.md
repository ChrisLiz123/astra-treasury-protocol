# Restricted Launch Public FAQ

## Is AstraTreasury live on mainnet?

AstraTreasury contracts are deployed on Base Mainnet in restricted operational mode.

## Is there a public token sale?

No.

## Is ASTP an investment product?

No. AstraTreasury does not promise returns.

## Is the treasury funded with real assets?

No real treasury funding is approved.

## Are staking or rewards live?

No.

## Are buybacks live?

No.

## Is autonomous execution live?

No.

## Is the mainnet execution queue enabled?

No. The mainnet execution queue remains disabled.

## What is available publicly?

The mainnet contract manifest, monitoring pages, alert status, incident summary, transparency index, and restricted launch status are publicly available.
