# DEX Liquidity Funding Transfer Safe Submission Approval

## Purpose

Record approval to submit/propose the verified funding-transfer Safe payload in a later step.

## Current status

Funding-transfer Safe payload verified: yes.

Safe submission approval: pending or recorded.

Safe submission: no.

Safe execution: no.

Treasury funds moved: no.

Token approval executed: no.

Liquidity added: no.

Position minted: no.

Public trading approved: no.

Full launch approved: no.

## What this approval can approve

- Proceeding to a later Safe submission/proposal step.
- Using the verified Safe payload hash.
- Using the verified source Safe, destination Safe, transfer calldata, and token amounts.

## What this approval does not approve

- Safe submission during this approval step.
- Safe execution.
- Treasury funds movement.
- Token approval execution.
- Liquidity addition.
- Position minting.
- Public trading.
- Buy page activation.
- Full launch.

## Rule

Safe submission approval is not Safe submission and is not Safe execution.
