# Mainnet Operational Boundaries

## Current boundary

AstraTreasury is deployed on Base Mainnet in restricted mode.

## Operational boundaries

- Do not fund the TreasuryVault with real treasury assets yet.
- Do not announce a public token sale.
- Do not activate staking, rewards, or buybacks.
- Do not enable autonomous execution.
- Do not run a mainnet execution queue without a separate approval milestone.
- Do not market ASTP as an investment product.

## Public status language

Use: Base Mainnet contracts deployed in restricted operational mode.

Do not use: launched token sale, yield product, passive income, guaranteed returns, or buyback guarantee.
