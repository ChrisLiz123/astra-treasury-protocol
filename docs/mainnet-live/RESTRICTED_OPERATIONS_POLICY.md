# Restricted Mainnet Operations Policy

## Status

AstraTreasury Base Mainnet contracts are deployed, but protocol operations remain restricted.

## Allowed now

- Public viewing of deployed contracts.
- Public viewing of BaseScan links.
- Public audit/governance/transparency pages.
- Governance Safe administrative review.
- Post-deployment verification.

## Not approved

- Public token sale.
- Real treasury funding.
- Staking or rewards launch.
- Buyback program.
- Autonomous execution.
- Mainnet execution queue activation.
- Mainnet paper-to-on-chain automation.

## Rule

Every restricted capability must have separate legal, security, governance, and operational approval before activation.
