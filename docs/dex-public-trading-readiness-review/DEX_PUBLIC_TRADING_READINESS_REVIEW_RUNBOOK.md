# DEX Public Trading Readiness Review Runbook

## Step 1

Confirm DEX liquidity post-execution verification is complete.

## Step 2

Run public trading readiness review.

## Step 3

Review pool liquidity, position token ID, and position ownership.

## Step 4

Confirm public trading, buy page activation, and full launch remain off.

## Step 5

Proceed to public trading approval only as a separate milestone.

## Rule

Do not activate trading links or buy pages in this milestone.
