# Auditor Selection Criteria

## Decision priorities

1. Quality and depth of Solidity/EVM review
2. Fit for treasury and governance workflow
3. Ability to review off-chain approval queues
4. Ability to review invariants, fuzzing, and stateful tests
5. Retest and remediation support
6. Mainnet readiness feedback
7. Timeline
8. Cost clarity
9. Public-report option

## Red flags

- No written scope
- No retest policy
- No clear timeline
- No named reviewers or reviewer-quality explanation
- Unwilling to review workflow assumptions
- Pushes toward mainnet before legal or audit gates

## Selection rule

Select only after at least three serious candidates have been compared or after documenting why fewer candidates were sufficient.
