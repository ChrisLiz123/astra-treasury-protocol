# Auditor Questionnaire

## Project summary

AstraTreasury Protocol v0.1.1 is a Base Sepolia testnet prototype for AI-assisted treasury governance.

## Questions for auditors

1. Can you audit Solidity contracts and the off-chain approval workflow together?
2. Can you review the SignalRegistry and ExecutionController interaction model?
3. Can you review TreasuryPolicy limits and bypass scenarios?
4. Can you review role-transfer and Safe governance planning?
5. Can you review our local stateful and randomized fuzz harnesses?
6. Can you provide mainnet readiness recommendations?
7. Do you provide a retest after remediations?
8. Can the final report be public?
9. What is your estimated timeline?
10. What is your pricing model?
11. What materials do you need before scoping?
12. Do you require a code freeze?
13. Do you review operational scripts and queue logic?
14. Do you review incident response and rollback runbooks?
15. Do you provide severity ratings and reproduction steps?
