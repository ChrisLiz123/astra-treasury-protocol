# DEX Liquidity Post-Execution Verification Runbook

## Step 1

Confirm execution-live status is complete.

## Step 2

Run post-execution verification.

## Step 3

Review pool liquidity, position token ID, and position owner.

## Step 4

Confirm public trading and full launch remain off.

## Step 5

Proceed to public trading readiness review or approval milestone.

## Rule

Do not enable public trading in this milestone.
