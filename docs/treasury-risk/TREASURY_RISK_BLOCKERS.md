# Treasury Risk Blockers

The following block real treasury funding:

- Treasury risk limits not approved.
- Source of funds not approved.
- Treasury Safe approval missing.
- Governance approval missing.
- Public disclosures not updated.
- Incident response drill not current.
- Approved asset list empty.
- Approved destination list empty.
- Funding transaction package missing.

## Rule

Audit/legal clearance alone does not authorize funding. Capability-specific treasury controls are still required.
