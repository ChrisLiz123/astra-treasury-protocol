# Treasury Asset Approval Policy

## Current status

No external real treasury assets are approved for funding.

## Required before approving an asset

- Asset identity verified.
- Contract address verified, if tokenized.
- Liquidity and counterparty risk reviewed.
- Legal/accounting treatment reviewed.
- Sanctions/compliance review completed.
- Treasury Safe approval recorded.
- Governance approval recorded.
- Public disclosure updated if required.

## Current approved external assets

None.

## Rule

No external treasury asset should be transferred until approved through this policy.
