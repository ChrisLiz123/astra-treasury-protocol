# Full Launch Approval

## Purpose

Record approval for AstraTreasury full launch after buy page activation, public trading live status, and liquidity verification.

## Current status after this milestone

Buy page activated: yes.

Public trading live: yes.

Liquidity added: yes.

Position minted on-chain: yes.

Full launch readiness review: complete.

Full launch approval: recorded.

Full launch approved: yes.

Global treasury funding approved/executed: no.

Full launch live finalization: no.

## What this approval can approve

- Formal full launch approval.
- Proceeding to a later full-launch-live/public-finalization milestone.

## What this approval does not do

- Approve global treasury funding.
- Execute global treasury funding.
- Create a separate full-launch-live finalization.

## Rule

Full launch approval is not global treasury funding approval.
