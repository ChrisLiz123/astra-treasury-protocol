# Legal Counsel Questionnaire

## Project summary

AstraTreasury Protocol v0.1.1 is a Base Sepolia testnet prototype for AI-assisted treasury governance.

## Questions for counsel

1. Can you review whether any planned ASTP distribution creates investment-contract risk?
2. Can you review public website, GitHub, and documentation language?
3. Can you review tokenomics and allocation language?
4. Can you review treasury buyback, burn, and liquidity-support language?
5. Can you review whether any future CVC business model creates money-transmission or custody obligations?
6. Can you review commodity and market-conduct risks?
7. Can you review consumer-protection disclosures?
8. Can you review tax/accounting questions directly or refer specialist counsel?
9. Can you provide written mainnet go/no-go comments?
10. What is your expected timeline?
11. What is your pricing model?
12. What deliverables do you provide?
13. What jurisdictions are covered?
14. What jurisdictions are not covered?
15. Can we share your final memo with auditors, investors, or partners if needed?
