# Legal Engagement Checklist

## Before engagement

- [ ] Counsel selected.
- [ ] Conflict check completed.
- [ ] Scope confirmed.
- [ ] Price confirmed.
- [ ] Timeline confirmed.
- [ ] Deliverables confirmed.
- [ ] Jurisdictions confirmed.
- [ ] Engagement letter reviewed.
- [ ] Communication channel created.

## Materials to provide

- Public website.
- GitHub repository.
- Legal review package.
- Tokenomics draft.
- Mainnet readiness checklist.
- Marketing language policy.
- Public disclaimer.
- Deployment manifest.
- Audit-readiness docs.

## Mainnet rule

Legal engagement does not authorize mainnet deployment.
