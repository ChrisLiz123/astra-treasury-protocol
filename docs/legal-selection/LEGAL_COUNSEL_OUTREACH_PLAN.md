# Legal Counsel Outreach Plan

## Purpose

Prepare AstraTreasury Protocol v0.1.1 for legal counsel outreach and selection.

## Current status

Network: Base Sepolia
Mainnet launched: no
Real treasury funds: no
Public token sale: no
Investment product: no
Testnet ASTP has no real monetary value

## Counsel categories to contact

- Crypto securities counsel
- Fintech / money-transmission counsel
- Digital-asset tax counsel
- Consumer-protection / marketing review counsel

## Review goals

- Securities-law review.
- Token distribution review.
- Treasury buyback and liquidity-support language review.
- Money-transmission / custody review.
- Commodity and market-conduct review.
- Tax and accounting issue review.
- Marketing and public-disclosure review.
- Mainnet legal go/no-go comments.

## Rule

No mainnet deployment, public token sale, real treasury funding, staking program, buyback program, or investor-facing marketing should occur before legal review is complete.
