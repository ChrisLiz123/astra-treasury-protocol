# Tokenomics Legal Review Checklist

## Current status

Tokenomics are testnet-only and not approved for mainnet.

## Review items

- [ ] Total supply reviewed.
- [ ] Treasury allocation reviewed.
- [ ] Team allocation reviewed.
- [ ] Vesting reviewed.
- [ ] Community rewards reviewed.
- [ ] Grants reviewed.
- [ ] Liquidity allocation reviewed.
- [ ] Buyback language reviewed.
- [ ] Burn language reviewed.
- [ ] Staking or rewards language reviewed.
- [ ] Public sale structure reviewed if ever proposed.
- [ ] Jurisdictional restrictions reviewed.
- [ ] Tax/accounting treatment reviewed.

## Mainnet rule

No public tokenomics launch until counsel signs off.
