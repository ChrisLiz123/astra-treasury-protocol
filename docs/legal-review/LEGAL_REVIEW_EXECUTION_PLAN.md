Prepare AstraTreasury Protocol v0.1.1 for formal legal review before any mainnet or real-money activity.

## Legal review goals

- Review securities-law risk.
- Review token distribution design.
- Review treasury buyback or liquidity-support language.

- Review consumer protection disclosures.
- Review tax and accounting questions.
- Review public website, GitHub, and marketing language.
