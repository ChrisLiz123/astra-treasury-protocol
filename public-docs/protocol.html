How AstraTreasury works

Protocol overview

AstraTreasury is a governed workflow for AI-assisted treasury signals, policy validation, Safe-based approval, and read-only public monitoring.

Mode: Restricted mainnet operation
Step 1

Signal generation

AI-assisted workflows can produce treasury signals or proposals for review.

Step 2

Policy validation

Signals are checked against treasury policy, asset approval, and operational controls.

Step 3

Human approval

Governance and execution workflows remain gated by Safe-based human approval.
